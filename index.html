I am Sourabh, co-founder and CTO of Uplimit. Uplimit is a live online upskilling platform to help businesses and individuals acquire the cutting-edge technical skills necessary to remain competitive in today’s market. The Uplimit platform features live online courses from top industry experts, a rich community of peers, and leverages technology to deliver high-quality education with real career outcomes at scale.

In my past life, I lead the personalization and data infrastructure teams at Neeva. Before that at Google Brain, I worked on Tensorflow. Prior to Google, I was at Coursera working on data infrastructure and data products. I’m also a Georgia Tech and BITS Pilani alumni.
